Jamie Cabezas is a fantasy and supernatural storyteller who loves exploring worlds where magic, mystery, and emotion collide.

From strange dreams to supernatural transformations, Jamie's stories dive into the moments when reality begins to change and characters must decide who they are becoming.

When not writing, Jamie enjoys reading fantasy, creating new worlds, and imagining the next unforgettable story.
